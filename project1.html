SUNDAY IN BROOKLYN





Sunday in Brooklyn is a neighborhood restaurant located in Brooklyn, New York. It specializes in American staples, specifically seasonal Brooklyn-style dishes. These range from brunch food like pancakes and shakshuka to soups, salads, and sandwiches. This restaurant has a simple and clean website design with black text on a white background. There are also green illustrations and buttons, adding a pop of color throughout the entire website. The highlight of the site design is the slideshow of high-quality photos on the homepage, showcasing the restaurant, food, and customers.

Special lunch menu

Certainly! Our restaurant's lunch menu is a culinary masterpiece that offers a delightful array of flavors and options to satisfy every palate.
From the moment diners open the menu, they are greeted with a diverse selection of appetizers that set the tone for a memorable meal.
Whether it's the tantalizing aroma of garlic-infused bruschetta or the creamy indulgence of spinach and artichoke dip, these appetizers promise a tantalizing start to the dining experience.
Moving on to the main courses, your lunch menu shines with a mix of classic comfort foods and inventive dishes that cater to a wide range of tastes.
Signature sandwiches, such as the mouthwatering pulled pork sliders or the gourmet turkey and avocado club, provide a satisfying midday option for those craving something familiar yet refined.
For those seeking a more substantial meal, your menu proudly presents a selection of entrees that showcase the chef's culinary prowess.
